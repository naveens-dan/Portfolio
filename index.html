My Story

I'm a creative developer and UI designer with over 6 years of experience crafting digital experiences that delight users and drive business growth. My unique blend of technical expertise and design sensibility allows me to bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality.

After graduating with a degree in Interactive Media Design, I worked with startups and established companies alike, helping them build products that users love. When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring photography, practicing minimalism, and seeking inspiration in nature and architecture.
