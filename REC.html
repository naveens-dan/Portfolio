📋 How to Use:

  • Configure quality, format, and capture area
  • Toggle audio and cursor options as needed
  • Click "Start Recording" and select what to capture
  • Use Pause/Resume during recording
  • Stop recording and choose export options
  • View recording history for easy access

⚙️ Quality Settings:

  • Ultra (8 Mbps): Best quality, larger files
  • High (5 Mbps): Great quality, good balance
  • Medium (3 Mbps): Good quality, smaller files
  • Low (1 Mbps): Basic quality, smallest files

💡 Recording Tips:

  • Tab Recording: Browser sharing bar will be minimized automatically
  • Clean Recording: Close unnecessary tabs and notifications
  • Best Quality: Set browser zoom to 100% before recording
  • Stable Connection: Ensure good performance during recording

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts:

Ctrl + Shift + R - Start/Stop Recording

Ctrl + Shift + P - Pause/Resume

Ctrl + Shift + D - Download Video

Esc - Stop Recording

? - Show/Hide Help
